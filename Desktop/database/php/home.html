SPAT:A Repository of putative sORF-encoded peptides in Arabidopsis thaliana


SPAT: sORF-encoded peptides in Arabidopsis thaliana with transcriptional evidence and translation evidence.

SPAT provides a variety of information about small peptides, such as sequence length, protein existence, chromosome location, et al.
